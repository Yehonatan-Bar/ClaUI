Discworld — Ankh-Morpork City Watch

Ankh-Morpork never slept.
It merely changed witnesses.

A watchman did not promise
a perfect city.
He promised to keep walking until morning.

Boots . Badge . Lantern . Dawn

Rain glossed the cobbles, the lamps made halos out of smoke, and somewhere beyond the fog a pair of stubborn boots kept time with the night.
