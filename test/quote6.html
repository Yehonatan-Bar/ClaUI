No theme may be played that hath not
its uttermost source in me,
nor can any alter the music in my despite.
For he that attempteth this shall be but
mine instrument in the devising
of things more wonderful,
which he himself hath not imagined.

Ilúvatar to Melkor
Ainûlind&aleg; — The Silmarillion
