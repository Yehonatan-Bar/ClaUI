The young priest said, "What is it that
your faith offers, then?"

And Granny Weatherwax looked at the sunset,
and at the skulls, and at the description
of what awaited the wicked, and said—

"Sin, young man, is when you treat
people as things.

Including yourself.

That's what sin is."

"It's a lot more complicated than that—"

"No. It ain't. When people say things
are a lot more complicated than that,
they means they're getting worried that
they won't like the truth."
