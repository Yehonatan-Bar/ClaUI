The Duel Before Angband

He passed over Dor-nu-Fauglith like a wind amid the dust,
and all that beheld his onset fled in amaze,

thinking that Oromë himself was come:
for a great madness of rage was upon him,
so that his eyes shone like the eyes of the Valar.

He came alone to Angband's gates, and he sounded
his horn, and smote once more upon the brazen doors,
and challenged Morgoth to come forth
to single combat.

Fingolfin, High King of the Noldor
The Gates of Angband — First Age, Year 456
