Discworld — Carpe Jugulum

She'd never needed to be liked.
She had something better than that,
something that went deeper
and lasted longer.

"And sin, young man, is when you
treat people as things."

"Including yourself.
That's what sin is."

The vampires had expected fear.
They had expected weakness. They had not
expected an old woman who held the dark
at bay by refusing to look away from it.

She was the other edge
of the scissors.

Headology was not magic. It was something
far more dangerous: it was knowing exactly
who you were, and never flinching from it.

Esmerelda “Granny” Weatherwax
Witch of Lancre
